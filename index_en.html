Who am I?

Hello! I'm Pedro Conrad Junior.

I develop my work focusing on Information Systems and I'm always searching for new challenges. I work with computing since 1996, when I was just developing systems to learn something new. I've worked with systems suport, computer assembly, home lecturing and in universities (also guiding students through their final graduation work) and lectured some training to developer and technology enthusiast groups.

Talking about systems development, I worked in desktop projects (in the beginning of 2000's using Visual Basic and Delphi, and most recently using C#); systems built for use with web browsers through HTML, CSS, Javascript and PHP. In the last couple years I'm working with Flutter, focusing mainly in mobile devices. I also work occasionally managing teams, helping to define development processes and software architecture, and modeling databases, creating and reviewing technical and user documentation, evaluating tools and reviewing code.

Throughout my journey I can safely say that I consider myself an 'eternal apprentice', as I'm always learning with organizations, their culture, technology and mainly, with people. I worked in several companies, but my last employers included three universities, where I worked as a teacher and a member of the technical staff, specially in the last two. This list includes Universidade da Região da Campanha(URCAMP) and Universidade Federal de Pelotas (UFPEL).

Currently, I work as an IT Analyst, developing software for Universidade Federal do Pampa (UNIPAMPA) since mid-2010.
