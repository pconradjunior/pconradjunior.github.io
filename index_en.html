Who am I

Hi! My name is Pedro Conrad Junior, and I work with Information Technologies for a long time. As an IT professional with 20+ years of experience, I have worked in several positions related to it, and I'm always looking for new challenges.

I've worked with hardware and systems support, in industries and companies such as banks, schools and also internet providers. I also taught classes at a university and mentored students, and acted as a facilitator in training and lectures for developers and technology enthusiasts.

Currently I work developing web (using PHP and Javascript) and mobile apps (Android/iOS). In my current position I use a lot of HTML, CSS, Javascript and PHP, but I've also been working with Flutter, a multiplatform technology, most used for mobile apps development.

Sometimes I also help teams by creating and reviewing documentation, evaluating tools and doing code reviews. I can act in developer, analyst or in application support positions, participating in different phases of the software development process, including architecture definition and database modelling.
