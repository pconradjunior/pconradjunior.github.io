Who am I

As an IT professional with over 20 years of experience, I have held various roles in the field and am always seeking new challenges.

I've worked in hardware and systems support, in industries and companies such as banks, factories, and internet service providers.

I was a university professor, guiding students in their final graduation projects and internships. I also facilitated training sessions and workshops for groups of developers and technology enthusiasts.

Currently, I work on developing web systems and mobile applications. I can work as a developer, analyst, or provide support for applications, participating in any phase of the software development process, including architecture definition and database structure modeling.

I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. In recent years, I have also been working with Flutter, a cross-platform technology mainly used for mobile apps development.

Occasionally, I assist teams by creating and reviewing system documentation, evaluating tools, and reviewing their code.
