Who am I

I am an Information Technology professional with over 20 years of experience, specializing in developing web and mobile applications and other systems.

Currently, I work as an Information Technology Analyst at Universidade Federal do Pampa (Unipampa), leading and participating in development projects using PHP, Javascript, and Flutter.

Throughout my career, I have accumulated solid skills in systems analysis, defining software architectures and standards, as well as configuring and updating application environments.

I am recognized for my ability to refine functional requirements and collaborate in defining data models and prototypes. I also consolidated my skills on turning requirements into functional software products.

I am passionate about technology and innovation, constantly seeking to learn and apply new tools and methodologies in my projects. I am looking for challenging opportunities to grow professionally and contribute significantly to the advancement of the Information Technology field.
