Who am I

I am an Information Technology professional with over 20 years of experience, specialized in developing web and mobile applications and other systems. Currently, I work as an Information Technology Analyst at Universidade Federal do Pampa (Unipampa), where I lead development projects using PHP, Javascript and Flutter.

Throughout my career, I have accumulated solid skills in systems analysis, defining software architectures and standards, as well as configuring and updating application environments. I am recognized for my ability to refine functional requirements and collaborate in defining data models and prototypes.

Previously, I played the role of Deputy Coordinator of the Systems Development team at Unipampa, managing projects and teams, in addition to contributing as part of the Information and Communication Technology management team.

My professional journey includes stints at the Universidade Federal de Pelotas (UFPel), where I worked as an Information Technology Technician, responsible for web development and administration of the Virtual Learning Environment (Moodle). Furthermore, my experience at the Universidade da Região da Campanha (URCAMP) consolidated my skills as a PHP Web Developer.

As an educator, I have had the privilege of teaching at different levels of education, from technical subjects for high school students to undergraduate courses in IT, covering topics such as Algorithms, Programming, Databases, and Artificial Intelligence.

I am passionate about technology and innovation, constantly seeking to learn and apply new tools and methodologies in my projects. I am looking for challenging opportunities that will allow me to continue growing professionally and contribute significantly to the advancement of the IT area.
